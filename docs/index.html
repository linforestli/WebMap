The 7 Colleges at the University of Toronto

UofT is distinguished by its unique collegiate system. This system is comprised of seven colleges, each with its own distinct character, traditions, and history, yet all are integral parts of the UofT community. These colleges include:

  • Innis College, known for its modernist architecture and active film society
  • New College, with a focus on innovation
  • St. Michael's College, rooted in the Catholic intellectual tradition
  • Trinity College, famous for its formal traditions and academic excellence
  • University College, celebrated for its inclusive spirit and gothic architecture
  • Victoria College, known for its strong arts and humanities programs
  • Woodsworth College, which emphasizes diversity

Each college provides a smaller community within the larger university, offering its students a unique blend of social, academic, and cultural experiences.
